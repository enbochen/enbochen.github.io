人之初 Origins of Being (2024)

a performance to explore the eternal cycles of creation and dissolution that define our cosmos of flesh and souls, both macroscopically and microscopically, through an evocative synthesis of soundscapes, movements, light, and shadows. It delves into the intricate interplay between humans and machines, reflecting on the quest for our origins as human beings.

Collaborate with: Sveta, Olya, Nekku, Nanny
