Enbo is a performer exploring the dialogue between natural and synthetic breath, silence, and resonance.

Their live performances combine extended voice with the psychoacoustic interplay of human organs and machines.

Through ritualistic hyper-presence, they investigate the thresholds between oblivious spectral whispers and visceral cries.
